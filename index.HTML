WEB

The World Wide Web (WWW, W3) is a worldwide information space where people can share information through computers connected to the Internet . Often referred to simply as the Web . This term is often used synonymously with the Internet, but is strictly a different concept. The web is just one service that works on the Internet, such as e-mail . However, since 1993, the web has occupied the absolute position of the Internet structure.
